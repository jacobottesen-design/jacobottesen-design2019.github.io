About me

I love to design, mentor, draw, camp, hike, travel, & grow a mustache.

The professional me

I am currently a senior at BYU-Idaho (Online) pursuing a BS in Web Design & Development and living in Salt Lake City. During my time in school, I’ve had the opportunity to test and refine my design process through real-world UX design experience. Previously, I was a UX design intern @IBM, FamilySearch, Workfront, and Boncom.

I’m also a firm believer that design should be done in the open allowing for early cross-collaboration. I also value user empathy, humility, learning from everyone, and designing with intentionality. I am always looking for future opportunities to grow.

The personal me

When I am not doing design work, you will find me eating Trader Joe's cookie butter, hiking, camping, traveling, or growing a mustache. I love learning new perspectives as travel and immerse myself in other cultures. Being in nature is one of my favorites was to unplug and relax.
